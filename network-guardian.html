Why now

The timing is real

Work is distributed, network choice is constant, and more people, apps, and AI systems now depend on networks they do not control.

Work is everywhere now

Remote work, travel, hybrid campuses, and constant mobility have turned network choice into a daily security decision.

Existing tools are reactive

VPNs act after connection. OS warnings stay generic. Enterprise tools protect network owners, not the person choosing the Wi-Fi.

AI agents need trust too

Apps and AI agents increasingly send credentials and sensitive context. They need a trust signal before they rely on the network.

Every scan improves the trust graph

Network Guardian is not just a scanning tool. Over time, it becomes a user-side trust layer for Wi-Fi infrastructure.

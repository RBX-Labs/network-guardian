About RBX Labs

RBX Labs is a founder-led AI studio built by Rishabh Banga. It exists to help companies move beyond AI experimentation and into practical execution.

The studio partners with founders, product teams, and operators to build AI products, workflows, and go-to-market systems that create real business value.

Every engagement is hands-on, execution-focused, and designed to produce outcomes teams can actually use. RBX Labs is built on a simple belief: AI becomes valuable when it is embedded into the way a company builds, operates, and grows.

Founder-led execution, kept close to the work

The practice spans AI products, workflows, go-to-market systems, trust infrastructure, and team enablement. The constant is direct involvement from the person shaping the work.

No layers, no handoffs, and no inflated strategy process. The work stays close to the problem and close to execution.
